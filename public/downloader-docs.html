Overview

Hyde Downloader is a native Windows application built to extract video and audio from virtually any website. It wraps the power of yt-dlp in a modern, easy-to-use interface.

How It Works

  1. Copy Link: Copy the URL of the video or audio you want to save.
  2. Paste: Open Hyde Downloader and click the "Paste Link" button.
  3. Select Format: Choose Video (MP4/MKV) or Audio (MP3/WAV).
  4. Download: Click Start. The file will be saved to your 'Downloads/Hyde' folder.
