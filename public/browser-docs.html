Usage

Hyde Browser provides a clean, intuitive interface for browsing the web while maintaining your privacy.

All privacy features are enabled by default, ensuring you're protected from the moment you start browsing.

Privacy & Security

Hyde Browser is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any browsing data, search history, or personal information.

All browsing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers for tracking or analytics purposes.

Contributing
