About Hyde

Restoring the internet's utility layer, one tool at a time.

Our Mission

"We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right, not a premium feature."

Hyde is dedicated to building software that is free from ads, trackers, and data harvesting. In an age where users are treated as products, Hyde stands as a counter-movement: building tools that serve you.
